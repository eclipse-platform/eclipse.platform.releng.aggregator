Installation Details for Plug-ins

You can browse detailed information about the individual plug-ins that compose your installation. This view is useful when the list of installed software or the feature detail is not enough. At times you want to know exactly which versions of which plug-ins are installed into your system, without having to know what feature contained the plug-in. To browse the plug-in details:

  1. Click Help > About and then click Installation Details... to open a dialog showing pages that provide more detail about your installation.
  2. Click the Plug-ins tab to see a list of the plug-ins that are installed in your configuration. The list includes each plug-in's name, provider, id, and version.
  3. Select a plug-in to see additional detail.
    • Click Legal Info to launch a browser that shows any legal information that may be provided with the plug-in.
    • Click Show Signing Info to view information about the signing certificate provided with the plug-in.
